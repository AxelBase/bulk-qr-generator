Introducing Bulk QR Code Generator

Introducing Bulk QR Code Generator

Published: November 22, 2025

Need to generate 500 event tickets with unique check-in links? Or 2,000 inventory tags with individual tracking URLs? Most online QR generators either limit you to 10 at a time, require uploads, or charge per batch.

Bulk QR Code Generator solves this — instantly, privately, and for free.

100% Client-Side. Zero Uploads.

This tool runs entirely in your browser using SvelteKit and the static adapter. When you drop a CSV file or paste data, nothing is sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device — not even temporarily.

After the first load, it works completely offline. Perfect for secure environments or when you're on a plane.

Blazing Fast Performance

Thanks to kjua for QR rendering and JSZip for packaging, this tool can generate 10,000+ QR codes in under 20 seconds on a modern laptop — all without freezing the browser.

Progressive generation with a live progress bar and memory-efficient blob handling ensures smooth performance even on lower-end devices.

Key Features

  • Supports PNG and crisp SVG output
  • Custom size (200–500px), colors, and error correction
  • Use any CSV column as filename
  • Add prefix/suffix to URLs automatically
  • Download everything as a clean ZIP
  • Works on mobile and desktop

Perfect For Real-World Use

Event organizers use it for personalized tickets. Warehouses generate asset labels. Cafés create WiFi access codes. Schools print student ID badges. All with a single CSV and one click.

No account. No limits. No tracking.
