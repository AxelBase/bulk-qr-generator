Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 22, 2025

Welcome to Bulk QR Code Generator — a completely client-side, offline-first web application. We are deeply committed to your privacy. Because this tool runs 100% in your browser, we do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data — ever.

Zero Data Collection

When you use this tool:

  • No CSV files are uploaded — they are parsed directly in your browser using PapaParse
  • No QR codes leave your device — they are generated with kjua and packaged with JSZip locally
  • No IP addresses, browser info, or usage analytics are recorded
  • No cookies, localStorage persistence, or tracking pixels are used

How Your Data Stays Private

Everything happens in memory or temporary Blob objects. When you:

  • Drop or paste a CSV → processed instantly by your CPU
  • Click “Generate” → QR codes rendered on canvas/SVG in your browser
  • Download ZIP → created and streamed directly from your device

Nothing touches any server. Even if you’re offline after the first load, the tool works perfectly.

No Third-Party Services

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Cloudflare Insights, or any external tracking. The only external resources are optional CDNs for Bootstrap and Bootstrap Icons — which do not track users.

Your Responsibility

Since all processing is local:

  • Do not input sensitive personal data if you don’t want it in browser history
  • Clear your browser cache if handling confidential CSVs
  • Use in private/incognito mode for maximum isolation

Children’s Privacy

This tool does not target children under 13 and collects no data from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the new effective date.

Your privacy is not negotiable.
That’s why we built a tool that needs none of it.
